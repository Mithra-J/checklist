Scheduling Tips

Prioritize Your Tasks

Focus on high-value activities first. For coding practice, start with the platform that aligns with your current goals.

Break Tasks into Smaller Steps

Instead of "solve 5 problems," try "solve 2 array problems, 2 string problems, and 1 DP problem."

Time Blocking

Schedule specific time slots for each platform. Example: 9-10 AM for LeetCode, 4-5 PM for CodeChef.

Active Recall & Spaced Repetition

Review previously solved problems regularly. Revisit difficult problems after 1 day, 3 days, and 1 week.

Track Progress Daily

Use this scheduler to mark completed tasks. Visual progress tracking boosts motivation and consistency.

Balance Learning Platforms

Don't focus on just one platform. Mix LeetCode (interview prep) with CodeChef (competitions) and Mimo (concepts).
